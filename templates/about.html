About Our Website

How This Website Works

Getting Started

It is HIGHLY RECOMMENDED to customize your profile to your likeness before selecting any of the recommendation options. However if you do choose to test these features please know that the website will produce results based on the following profile:

Budget: $3.00-$20.00, no allergens, no preferred food items, and no preferred tastes

How Is My Rating Quantified?

This system adds points in accordance to your profile's matches to each food's flavor/ingredients/cost relative to your profile's information and budget. A numerical value is calculated based on the number of matches with the food item's information and is recalcuated with each profile update.
Is Based on a 0-5 scale:

3 or more (Relatively high match)
1.5-2.5 (Decent match)
1 or less (Unlikely match)

(NOTE) If you are getting low scores, this could be because the profile needs to be more detailed, ie you add more information, which results in more matches. The best course of action for ingredients is to keep it generic (ie bread not sourdough bread or cheese not pepperjack)

Using Our Search Services

For Search: the results will be based entirely on your search query, it will be provide results filtering out results from your allergens and your saved list.
For Random Recommendation: Will recommend food items randomly, filtering out results based on allergens and your saved list. If you choose to leave to another part of the website your progress is reset.
For Recommendation by Restaurant: This will populate with results for a particular restaurant, filtering out results based on allergens and your saved list. If you choose to leave to another part of the website, progress is reset (also is reset if you reach the end of the list)
